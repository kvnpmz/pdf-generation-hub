Flare-Up

Food List

Eat

  • White rice
  • Plain pasta
  • White bread (no seeds)
  • Plain crackers
  • Cooked carrots
  • Cooked zucchini (peeled)
  • Cooked potatoes (peeled)
  • Cooked green beans
  • Cooked spinach
  • Cooked beets
  • Canned peaches (no syrup)
  • Canned pears (no syrup)
  • Applesauce
  • Ripe bananas
  • Watermelon (seedless)
  • Eggs (boiled/scrambled)
  • Chicken breast (skinless)
  • Turkey breast
  • Lean ground beef
  • Broth-based soups
  • Bone broth
  • Tofu
  • White fish (baked/steamed)
  • Smooth peanut butter

Limit

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Brown rice
  • Quinoa
  • Sweet potato (peeled)
  • Cucumber (peeled, no seeds)
  • Tomato (peeled, no seeds)
  • Avocado
  • Steamed broccoli
  • Steamed cauliflower
  • Lettuce
  • Melon
  • Blueberries (no skin)
  • Raspberries (strained)
  • Cooked apples
  • Low-fiber cereals
  • Baked chicken with light seasoning
  • Poached fish
  • Scrambled eggs with herbs
  • Greek yogurt (plain)
  • Almond milk (unsweetened)
  • Low-fat cheese
  • Mashed lentils
  • Cooked squash
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Cooked oats
  • Plain rice cakes
  • Light peanut butter
  • Coconut water

Avoid

  • Corn
  • Popcorn
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)
  • Seeds (chia, flax, sesame)
  • Raw vegetables (carrots, celery, broccoli)
  • Cabbage
  • Beans (black, kidney, chickpeas)
  • Lentils (whole)
  • Dried fruits
  • Apples (with skin)
  • Berries with seeds
  • Grapes (with skin)
  • Prunes
  • Coconut (shredded, oil)
  • Spicy foods
  • Fried foods
  • Sausage
  • Bacon
  • Processed meats
  • Pizza
  • Chips
  • Pastries
  • Ice cream
  • Carbonated drinks
  • Caffeinated coffee
  • Alcohol
