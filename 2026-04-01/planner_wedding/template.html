Wedding Planning Checklist

12–18 Months Before

Vision + Budget

  • Define your wedding vision: theme, size, vibe, and location (local or destination).
  • Set your total budget and prioritize spending (venue, food, dress, photography).
  • Create a shared Google Sheet or Notion workspace for planning.

Guest List + Date

  • Draft your preliminary guest list.
  • Choose potential wedding dates and check with key guests/family.

Venue + Planner

  • Research and visit venues (ceremony and reception).
  • Book your venue early – this often determines your date.
  • Consider hiring a wedding planner or coordinator (full-service or day-of).

Vendors

  • Photographer & Videographer
  • Caterer
  • Hair & Makeup Artist
  • Decor/Styling Team
  • DJ or Band
  • Florist
9–12 Months Before

Bridal Party + Attire

  • Choose your bridal party (maid of honor, best man, bridesmaids, groomsmen).
  • Begin shopping for wedding dress/outfit – allow time for fittings and alterations.
  • Research attire for bridesmaids, groomsmen, and parents.

Vendors (Book Key Ones)

  • Book photographer, videographer, caterer, and entertainment.
  • Hire florist, décor stylist, or event designer.
  • Reserve officiant.

Accommodation

  • Reserve hotel room blocks for guests (especially for destination weddings).
  • Plan transportation if venues are far apart.
6–9 Months Before

Details + Design

  • Order save-the-dates and send them out.
  • Finalize menu options and schedule tastings.
  • Choose color palette, flowers, and décor theme.

Fashion & Beauty

  • Choose bridal shoes and accessories.
  • Book hair and makeup artists for trials.
  • Begin skincare/haircare prep routines.

Wedding Website

  • Create a wedding website with RSVP, travel info, dress code, and registry details.

Entertainment & Extras

  • Book any extras like a photo booth, fireworks, or live performers.
4–6 Months Before

Attire & Fittings

  • Schedule your first dress fitting.
  • Select groom and groomsmen outfits.
  • Finalize bridesmaids' dresses and accessories.

Stationery

  • Design and order invitations.
  • Order ceremony programs, menus, and place cards.

Vows & Ceremony

  • Begin writing your vows.
  • Finalize your ceremony structure and readings.
  • Confirm details with your officiant.

Registry

  • Finalize your gift registry (or honeymoon fund).
2–4 Months Before

Final Details

  • Send wedding invitations (8–10 weeks before the date).
  • Finalize menu and drink selections.
  • Confirm floral arrangements and décor setup.
  • Create a wedding day timeline (from prep to last dance).
