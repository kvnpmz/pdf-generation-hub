CARNIVORE DIET Grocery List

Meats
Red Meat
  • Beef (steak, ground beef, roasts, ribs)
  • Lamb (chops, ground lamb, leg of lamb)
  • Pork (pork chops, pork shoulder, bacon, ribs)
  • Venison (steaks, ground venison)
  • Bison (steaks, ground bison)
  • Goat (goat chops, stew meat)
Poultry
  • Chicken (whole chicken, thighs, breasts, wings)
  • Turkey (whole turkey, ground turkey, turkey breast)
  • Duck (whole duck, duck breast)
  • Quail (whole quail)
Organ Meats
Variety Meats
  • Liver (beef, chicken, lamb, pork)
  • Kidney (beef, lamb)
  • Heart (beef, chicken, lamb)
  • Brain (beef, lamb)
  • Tongue (beef, pork)
  • Bone marrow (beef marrow bones)
  • Sweetbreads (thymus or pancreas of lamb or calf)
Dairy (If Tolerated)
Dairy Products
  • Butter (unsalted, grass-fed if possible)
  • Heavy cream (full-fat, no additives)
  • Cheese (aged cheeses like cheddar, parmesan, gouda)
  • Cream cheese (full-fat, no additives)
  • Greek yogurt (full-fat, plain, no added sugar)
Eggs
Eggs
  • Chicken eggs (whole, pasture-raised)
  • Duck eggs
  • Quail eggs
Seafood
Fish
  • Salmon (wild-caught, fillets, steaks)
  • Tuna (fresh or canned in water/oil)
  • Mackerel
  • Sardines (fresh or canned in water/oil)
  • Cod
  • Haddock
  • Trout
Shellfish
  • Shrimp
  • Crab
  • Lobster
  • Scallops
  • Oysters
  • Mussels
Bone Broth & Gelatin
Bone Broth & Gelatin
  • Bone broth (beef, chicken, pork; without additives)
  • Gelatin powder (unflavored, from grass-fed sources)
Processed Meats
Processed Meats
  • Bacon (sugar-free, nitrate-free)
  • Sausages (sugar-free, additive-free)
  • Deli meats (roast beef, turkey breast, chicken breast, additive-free)
Fats
Animal Fats
  • Beef tallow
  • Pork lard
  • Duck fat
  • Chicken fat (schmaltz)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Butter (unsalted, grass-fed if possible)
Supplements (If Needed)
Carnivore Diet Supplements
  • Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium supplements if needed)
  • Vitamin D3 (if not getting sufficient sunlight)
  • Omega-3 fish oil (if not consuming fatty fish regularly)
  • Collagen peptides or protein (from grass-fed sources)
Seasonings
Minimal Seasonings
  • Salt (preferably sea salt or Himalayan pink salt)
  • Pepper (if tolerated)
  • Herbs (limited use: fresh rosemary, thyme, oregano)
  • Spices (limited use: garlic, onion powder)
Miscellaneous
Carnivore Items
  • Pork rinds (unseasoned, no additives)
  • Liver pate (made from pure liver, no added sugars or fillers)
  • Beef jerky (sugar-free, nitrate-free)
  • Fish roe (caviar, salmon roe)
