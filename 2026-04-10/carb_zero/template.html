Zero Carb

Diet Guide

GOAL: Eliminate carbohydrates to promote fat burning, stabilize blood sugar, and reduce inflammation.
NOTE: Most "zero-carb" foods actually have trace carbs (under 1g per serving), but they're generally considered carb-free for practical purposes.
FOODS TO EAT

1. MEAT AND POULTRY

Allowed:

BEEF (STEAK, GROUND BEEF, RIBS), LAMB, PORK (CHOPS, BACON, HAM), VEAL, VENISON, CHICKEN, TURKEY, DUCK, AND GOOSE.

WHY: 100% protein and fat – zero carbs naturally.
TIP: Choose unprocessed meats (avoid glazed, breaded, or cured with sugar).

2. FISH & SEAFOOD

ALLOWED:

SALMON, SARDINES, MACKEREL, TUNA, TROUT, HADDOCK, COD, HALIBUT, SHRIMP, CRAB, LOBSTER, CLAMS, AND SCALLOPS.

WHY: Excellent protein and omega-3 sources with zero carbs.
TIP: Avoid breaded or battered seafood.

3. EGGS

ALLOWED:

WHOLE EGGS, EGG WHITES, AND YOLKS — BOILED, SCRAMBLED, POACHED, OR FRIED IN BUTTER.

WHY: Virtually zero carbs, nutrient-dense, and easy to digest.

4. FATS & OILS

ALLOWED:

BUTTER, GHEE, LARD, TALLOW, OLIVE OIL, COCONUT OIL, AVOCADO OIL, AND MCT OIL.

WHY: Pure fats contain zero carbs and provide energy on low-carb diets.
FOODS TO AVOID

1. SUGARY FOODS

AVOID:

CANDY, CHOCOLATE, DESSERTS, PASTRIES, CAKES, AND SYRUPS.

WHY: High sugar = high carbs.

2. GRAINS & STARCHES

AVOID:

BREAD, PASTA, RICE, OATS, CORN, CEREAL, CRACKERS, AND ANYTHING MADE FROM FLOUR.

WHY: Major carb sources.

3. FRUITS

AVOID:

ALL FRUITS (BANANAS, APPLES, BERRIES, MANGOES, PINEAPPLES, ETC.).

WHY: Contain natural sugars (fructose).

4. VEGETABLES (EXCEPT LEAFY GREENS, IF STRICTLY ZERO)

AVOID:

POTATOES, SWEET POTATOES, CARROTS, PEAS, BEANS, CORN, AND ANY STARCHY VEGETABLES.

WHY: Contain moderate to high carbs.

5. LEGUMES

AVOID:

BEANS, LENTILS, CHICKPEAS, PEANUTS, AND SOY PRODUCTS (UNLESS MINIMAL-CARB VERSIONS).

WHY: High in carbohydrates.
